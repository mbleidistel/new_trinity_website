How is the Integrated Post and Panel (IPP) System Energy Efficient?

The patented IPP system combines the strength and beauty of Post and Beam construction with the energy efficiency of Polyurethane Structural Insulated Panels.
  • At over R-7.5 per inch, the IPP system exceeds the newest building code regulations
  • Our insulation rates at R28+ for 4.5-inch walls and R40+ for 6.5-inch roof panels
  • Airtight technology delivers substantial comfort and savings
  • Polyurethane insulation reduces sound penetration
  • Polyurethane insulation results in greatly reduced energy to heat or cool a building
  • The Post and Beam design uses less timber (and no old growth timber)
  • The OSB Panel skins recycle wood chips
  • All materials are recyclable and longer lasting
  • Adhesive property of our insulation penetrates directly into the OSB or Magnesium Oxide Board skins
  • Polyurethane outperforms the old Polystyrene SIPS whose adhesive can delaminate
  • Our insulation is free of CFCs, VOCs and HFCs, resulting in one of the greenest foams available today.
